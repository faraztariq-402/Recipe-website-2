Chicken Karahi

Ingredients

  • 1 chicken,cut in small pieces
  • 1/2 cup oil
  • 2 tbsp,ginger garlic paste
  • 5-6 tomatoes,chopped
  • 1.5 tsp.salt
  • 1 tsp. all spices powder

Method

  • Heat oil in a work and saute garlic and ginger paste in it.Add chicken and cook it for 3-4 minutes on high flame
  • Add tomatoes,chilli powder and salt
  • After 5 minutes add yogurt,green chillies,cumin seeds,methi and all powders
  • Serve hot with chapattis
